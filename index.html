End-to-End Network Engineering & Operations

Drive Test & Optimization

The Challenge: Network operators face coverage gaps, dropped calls, and degraded data throughput that directly impact subscriber experience and churn rates.

Our Methodology: We deploy multi-technology drive test rigs with real-time KPI capture across 2G, 3G, 4G, and 5G layers. Our RF engineers correlate field data with OSS/BSS analytics to isolate root causes and prioritize optimization actions.

The Impact: Improved MOS scores, reduced dropped call rates by up to 40%, and increased data throughput — translating directly to higher ARPU and lower subscriber churn.

RF Engineering

The Challenge: Multi-vendor, multi-technology RF environments create interference, handover failures, and capacity bottlenecks that degrade network performance.

Our Methodology: Our RF planning team uses advanced propagation modeling tools and spectrum analysis to design optimized cell layouts, tune antenna parameters, and manage interference across VoLTE, LTE, WCDMA, and GSM networks.

The Impact: Seamless mobility management, improved cell-edge throughput, and reduced inter-frequency handovers — ensuring consistent QoS for high-value enterprise and consumer subscribers.

Network Optimization

The Challenge: As subscriber density grows, networks experience congestion, latency spikes, and resource underutilization — leading to SLA breaches and revenue leakage.

Our Methodology: We apply a data-driven optimization framework combining PM/FM analytics, traffic profiling, and machine-learning-backed parameter tuning. Our approach covers capacity planning, load balancing, and feature activation across the entire RAN and core.

The Impact: Up to 30% improvement in resource utilization, reduced RRC setup failures, and extended network lifecycle — deferring capital expenditure while maintaining SLA compliance.

Microwave Transmission

The Challenge: Fiber backhaul is not always feasible in remote or geographically challenging terrain, creating connectivity dead zones for cell towers and enterprise sites.

Our Methodology: We design and deploy carrier-grade point-to-point and point-to-multipoint microwave links with adaptive modulation, space diversity, and automatic transmit power control. Every link is engineered to meet 99.999% availability targets.

The Impact: Ultra-reliable backhaul delivering multi-gigabit throughput with sub-5ms latency — enabling rapid tower deployment and enterprise connectivity where fiber cannot reach.

WiMAX & Broadband

The Challenge: Rural and semi-urban regions lack fixed broadband infrastructure, limiting digital inclusion and enterprise expansion into underserved markets.

Our Methodology: We architect end-to-end WiMAX and fixed wireless broadband networks — from spectrum planning and base station deployment to CPE installation and NOC-managed service delivery. Our deployments prioritize coverage density and subscriber scalability.

The Impact: High-speed broadband access delivered to previously unserved areas, enabling new revenue streams for ISPs and supporting government digital transformation mandates with measurable connectivity KPIs.

Telecom Training

The Challenge: The telecom industry faces a widening skills gap — fresh engineers lack hands-on exposure to multi-vendor, multi-technology environments, slowing deployment velocity.

Our Methodology: We offer structured, industry-certified training programs covering RF planning, drive testing, optimization, and network management. Curricula are co-developed with operator partners and delivered through live equipment labs, not simulations.

The Impact: Job-ready engineers who reduce onboarding time by 50% and contribute to live projects from day one — strengthening your workforce pipeline and reducing dependency on external hiring.
